College of Hunters

Enrich Yourself

If you wanna become a top tier hunter in your area and be efficient with all the skills you have made look no further than Hunters Community College. We have made superior accomplishments in the past 5 years of upkeep in comparision to other places with the same resources. These accomplishments include training young and energetic hunters to become full fledged solo hunters with all the knowledge to survive by oneself this includes: cooking, hunting, and building.

Cheap and Affordable

If you came here expecting some huge amount of money needed just to learn the basics then you will be glad to hear we do a cheap monthly charge of only 10$. With only that you can take up to two of our courses at once. We also have a system in place for if you can not make payments on time, we will accept your hard work to help other hunters in lower ranks with your free time. Essentially paying us with your hard work and dedication.
